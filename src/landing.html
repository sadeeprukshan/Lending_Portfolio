Everything a lender needs

Built specifically for the way lending businesses actually work — brokers, creditors, documents, and monthly interest cycles.

⬡
Loan management
Create and manage all loan facilities. Track capital, interest rate, payment day, and loan status from a single clean interface.
◎
Broker network
Register your brokers, track which loans they arranged, and view their total capital arranged and monthly income contribution.
₨
Repayment tracking
Record interest payments monthly. View repayment status by broker, grouped by pay day, with period date filters.
📎
Document vault
Upload promissory notes, ID copies, mortgage documents, transfer receipts, and other documents. Multiple files per holder.
◈
Portfolio dashboard
KPI cards, broker charts, goal progress toward your monthly income target, and a compound reinvestment projection.
◍
Customer registry
Maintain a full customer (creditor) registry linked by NIC number to their loans, broker, and uploaded ID documents.
